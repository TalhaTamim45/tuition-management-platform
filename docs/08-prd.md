# Tuition Management Platform - Product Requirements Document (PRD)

## Product Vision
To build a trusted, transparent, and direct web-based matching platform that empowers guardians to find qualified tutors and enables tutors to acquire jobs without intermediary exploitation.

## MoSCoW Prioritization

### Must Have (MVP)
- Role-based user authentication (Admin, Tutor, Guardian).
- Tutor profile generation (Institution, salary, subjects, experience).
- Guardian job posting (Class, subjects, salary, location, preferred gender).
- Job application workflow (Apply, accept, reject).
- Basic Admin Dashboard (Moderate users and posts).

### Should Have
- Ratings and reviews system.
- Basic search filters (Filter jobs by class, salary, location).

### Could Have
- Automatic email notifications when a tutor applies.
- Verification badges for tutors who upload certificates.

### Won't Have (This Release)
- In-app chat messaging.
- Online payment processing.
