# Tuition Management Platform - User Personas

## Persona 1: The Seeking Guardian
- **Name:** Mrs. Nasrin
- **Age:** 42
- **Occupation:** Housewife / Parent
- **Needs:** Needs a reliable male/female tutor for her son who is in Class 9 (Science).
- **Frustrations:** Facebook groups are spammy. Agencies charge too much and send unqualified tutors.
- **Goal:** Quickly post a job, view qualified applicant profiles, and select one based on ratings.

## Persona 2: The Student Tutor
- **Name:** Fahim Ahmed
- **Age:** 21
- **Occupation:** CSE Student at BUET
- **Needs:** Needs a part-time tuition job in Mirpur to cover his living expenses.
- **Frustrations:** Cannot afford the 50% commission fee charged by local agencies.
- **Goal:** Filter available jobs by location and salary, apply with one click, and track application status.
