# Tuition Management Platform - Stakeholder Analysis

## Stakeholder Registry

| Stakeholder ID | Name | Role | Interest | Influence | Key Requirements |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| SH-01 | Guardians / Parents | Clients | High | High | Verified tutors, transparent reviews, easy posting, safety. |
| SH-02 | Tutors | Service Providers | High | Medium | Direct access to jobs, no high commission fees, clear application tracking. |
| SH-03 | Platform Administrator | Operations | Medium | High | Monitoring tools, abuse control, system statistics, user moderation. |
| SH-04 | Faculty Evaluators | Academic Quality | High | High | Clean database structure, responsive UI, proper documentation, role-based security. |

## Stakeholder Engagement Strategy
- **Guardians:** Focus on easy-to-use interfaces since they may not be tech-savvy.
- **Tutors:** Focus on mobile responsiveness since most university students browse on smartphones.
- **Administrators:** Focus on dashboard speed and easy user-blocking interfaces.
