# Tuition Management Platform - User Journeys

## Journey 1: Guardian Posting a Job and Hiring
1. **Discovery:** Guardian registers/logs in to the platform.
2. **Action:** Clicks "Create Tuition Post" and fills in class, subjects, salary (8000 BDT), Mirpur location, and offline mode.
3. **Wait:** Receives applications from interested tutors.
4. **Decision:** Reviews tutor profiles, selects Fahim (BUET student, 5-star rating), and clicks "Accept".
5. **Closure:** System marks the post as "Closed" and reveals contact details.

## Journey 2: Tutor Finding a Job
1. **Search:** Tutor logs in and goes to "Tuition Jobs".
2. **Filter:** Filters by "Mirpur" and "Math/Physics".
3. **Apply:** Finds Mrs. Nasrin's post, writes a short application message, and clicks "Apply".
4. **Notification:** Sees application status updated to "Accepted" once the guardian approves.
