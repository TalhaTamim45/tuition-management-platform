# Tuition Management Platform - Project Overview

## Executive Summary
The Tuition Management Platform is a web-based portal designed to connect tutors with students and guardians in Bangladesh. By replacing informal, unorganized channels like Facebook groups and local agencies, the platform establishes a structured, secure, and transparent ecosystem for tutoring arrangements.

## Project Background
In Bangladesh, tutoring is a highly popular supplementary education method. However, finding qualified tutors or reliable tutoring jobs relies heavily on word-of-mouth, unverified Facebook posts, or intermediary agencies charging exorbitant matching fees (often 50% to 100% of the first month's salary). The Tuition Management Platform addresses these inefficiencies by offering direct communication, profile verification, and structured tuition postings.

## Business Problem
1. **Lack of Trust & Verification:** Guardians struggle to verify tutor credentials, leading to safety and educational quality concerns.
2. **High Intermediary Fees:** Traditional tuition media/agencies exploit both parties by charging high commissions.
3. **Fragmented Search:** Tutors and guardians waste significant time browsing through irrelevant or duplicate postings across multiple Facebook groups.

## Proposed Solution
A unified, web-based platform supporting role-based access:
- **Tutors** can build verified profiles, search filtered tuition jobs, and apply directly.
- **Guardians** can post tuition requirements, review applicants, and select the best match.
- **Admins** can monitor the platform, verify users, and remove fake posts.

## Project Scope
- **In Scope (MVP):** Registration and login, Tutor profile management, Tuition post creation/management, Application flow (Apply, Accept/Reject), Admin dashboard (User & Post management).
- **Out of Scope (Future releases):** In-app chat, payment gateway integration, automatic location-based matching, and tutor background check automation.
