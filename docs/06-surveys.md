# Tuition Management Platform - Survey Results

## Survey Demographics
- **Respondents:** 45 university students (Tutors) and 15 parents (Guardians).
- **Location:** Mirpur, Uttara, and Dhanmondi (Dhaka).

## Core Questions & Responses

1. **How do you currently find tuition/tutors?**
   - Facebook Groups: 60%
   - Personal contacts/relatives: 30%
   - Agencies: 10%

2. **What is the most frustrating part of using agencies?**
   - Excessive commission fees: 80%
   - Lack of responsiveness: 15%
   - Inaccurate matches: 5%

3. **Would you use a platform that offers free/low-cost direct matching?**
   - Yes: 98%
   - No: 2%
