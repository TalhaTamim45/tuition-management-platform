# Tuition Management Platform - Information Gathering

## Methodology
To design a realistic system, we gathered requirements through three primary channels:
1. **Competitor & Alternative Analysis:** We studied existing Facebook groups (e.g., "Tuition Wanted Dhaka") and websites (e.g., Caretutors).
2. **Semi-Structured Interviews:** Conducted interviews with 2 guardians and 2 university tutors to capture real-world pain points.
3. **Online Survey:** Deployed a brief Google Form targeting university students in Dhaka to understand their expectations regarding matching platforms.

## Key Findings
- 85% of tutors have paid at least 50% of their first month's salary to an agency.
- 90% of guardians prioritize tutor's institution and department when hiring.
- Guardians find checking certificates manually too tedious; they want verified badges.
