# Tuition Management Platform - Interview Summaries

## Interview 1: Guardian (Mrs. Rahima, Dhaka)
- **Pain Point:** "I hired a tutor from Facebook who claimed to be from DU, but he couldn't teach basic math. I had no way to complain or verify."
- **Desired Feature:** Review and rating system for tutors.

## Interview 2: Tutor (Tanvir, DU Student)
- **Pain Point:** "I paid 4,000 BDT matching fee to a media agent. The student canceled after two classes, and the media refused to refund my money."
- **Desired Feature:** Direct matching without agents.
